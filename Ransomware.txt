Topic: Ransomware
URL: https://github.com/amiroooamiran/Malware-with-python#ransomware
Description: Ransomware in Python refers to malicious software designed to encrypt a user's files, rendering them inaccessible. Perpetrators then demand a ransom, often in cryptocurrency, for the decryption key. These Python-based attacks exploit vulnerabilities, emphasizing the importance of robust cybersecurity measures and regular software updates.
